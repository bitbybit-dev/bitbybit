# Installing 3D Bits App

The easiest way to install the 3D Bits app on your Shopify store is through the [**Shopify App Store**](https://apps.shopify.com/3d-bits-1).

[![Install 3D Bits app for Shopify](/img/3d-bits/tutorials/3d-bits-install.jpg)](https://apps.shopify.com/3d-bits-1)

## Installation Steps

### 1. Approve Permissions

After clicking install, you'll be asked to approve permissions for the app to access your store.

![Approve Permissions](/img/3d-bits/tutorials/approve-permissions.jpg)

### 2. Select a Subscription Plan

Next, choose one of our subscription plans: **Base**, **Standard**, or **Pro**. Note that prices are subject to change. [Read more about subscription plans](/learn/3d-bits/plans/subscription-plans)

### 3. Approve Subscription Charge

After selecting your plan, you'll need to approve the subscription charge in Shopify.

### 4. Installation Complete

Once approved, your trial period will begin and you'll be redirected to the 3D Bits admin dashboard.

![Installation Complete](/img/3d-bits/tutorials/3d-bits-installation-success.jpg)

You're now ready to start setting-up 3D Bits app for your Shopify store!
